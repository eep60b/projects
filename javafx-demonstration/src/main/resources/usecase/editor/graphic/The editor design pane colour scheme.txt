Overall: The colours are fixed and unchangeable.


100. The background (any unoccupied area) colour is in black.

110. The area colour is in light grey if not editable. It is in white if it is editable.

120. The sub-area will be shown by a frame (its border) with thin solid line as the border.

130. The location will be shown as light green colour if it is editable.

200. The grid is shown as broken line in a dark grey colour.

300. The occupied area (location) is in light green colour.

400. The border is in black colour.

500. The vegetation is in dark green colour.

600. The followers and fruits are in yellow, red.

700. The fence is in brown colour.

800. The brick is in red colour.
