
Design pane (Ground, Level 1) -- The pane which other objects are added. 
                        The ground is the whole design pane area which has a fixed dimension of 100 m X 100 m.
                        It is implemented as a StackPane

The back ground chart (Level 2) can be used add ruler and grid (implemented as Line Chart)

Area (Level 2) -- Garden Area, Farm Area, Patio Area, Indoor Area, Balcony Area, Customer Area
